  GENERATE PLANNING QUEUE PHASE
  ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  ORIENT ──────► REFINE ──────► PHASE_SHIFT
  (generates                    (generate_planning_queue → planning)
   plan-queue.json)

  Notes:
  - ORIENT: auto-generates <state_dir>/plan-queue.json from completed specs
  - REFINE: architect reviews, reorders, edits the file; validates on advance
  - PHASE_SHIFT: consumes plan queue, transitions to planning ORIENT
  - Can be skipped entirely: forgectl advance --from <file> at specifying PHASE_SHIFT
